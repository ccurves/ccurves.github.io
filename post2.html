Voice Controlled News App

Tori (Voice Controlled News App)

This is a React based application where users can converse with an AI and get the latest news from around the world. Using just their voice, the user can search for news by categories, by terms and by sources.

I made use of Alan AI for the voice control capabilities, Alan receives the command and through the help of a News API searches the web for the latest news and reads it out to the user. It can also hold small talks with the user.

I made use of News.org as the News API, my original idea for this application was to have the user interact with Alan in ‘Pidgin English’ but after some research I couldn’t find any News API or News Source that has its news in that language. Although that gives me an idea for a future project (i.e A Pidgin News API) I had use English.
